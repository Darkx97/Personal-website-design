Story of my life

5 years old :

A kid who's been left by his father in a " kindergarten" to start my interesting life as a student , I wasn't the braves kid , so when I saw my father leaving I started crying , as a kid I didn't understand , my father has to leave , I have to stay here , after a while I was used to that it was totally fine , I wasn't the smartest kid , I may have been one of the dumbest . things that would embarrass other ppl to talk about is , the fact that I , didn't use the places bathrooms and tried to hold on until the end of the school day wish wasn't successfully most the time . I had this problem haunting me for way longer than anyone should have it . and I think I may have been dum compared to the other kids , witch maybe what started my hell during school life , being bullied and out numbered , and getting hit by the other kids in the school . i was a small kid compared to the others witch can make you an ez target if you don't make friends , witch I didn't I also lacked the ability to socializes with the other kids , that made it much harder to socialize with kids in next years to come , while having to deal with problems like bullies and being physically attacked most my days there . at some point of my life maybe when I was 6 years old and in the 1st grade , my father bought a computer , I was exited for simple reasons , games on the computer , witch I loved wasting my time on them more than anything else , made me forget about the hard time im having in school , feeling like an idiot , weak , out numbered , and dealing the fact that I still had trouble with using the bathrooms in the school , my excuse for most of that was that they were un clean , filled with spiders witch I still fear to this day , and even at this point I didn't really have a friend . it was just me the computer and the games.

its here where I made my first friend , who is my best friend to this day . for the first time I feel like I can talk to someone , tell them anything without being hurt by them , I still had to deal with getting bullied and hurt from time to time but for me it was worth it to see that one person . we fight sometimes but we still come back friends the next day this happens , felt like every time we fight our friendship is some what more than before .
